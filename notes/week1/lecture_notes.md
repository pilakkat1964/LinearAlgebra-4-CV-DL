## Key Concepts
1. **Vectors and Scalars**:
   - Scalar: A single number (e.g., temperature, speed).
   - Vector: A collection of numbers (e.g., a force with magnitude and direction).
   - Visualization: Arrows marked in 2D or 3D space.

2. **Operations on Vectors**:
   - Addition/Subtraction: \( \mathbf{u} + \mathbf{v} \).
   - Scalar Multiplication: \( c \cdot \mathbf{v} \).

3. **Dot Product and Norms**:
   - Dot Product: \( \mathbf{u} \cdot \mathbf{v} = \|\mathbf{u}\| \|\mathbf{v}\| \cos\theta \).
   - Norm: Measurement of vector length \( \|\mathbf{v}\| \ = \sqrt{v_1^2 + v_2^2 + \dots} \).
