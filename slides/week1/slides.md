# Week 1 Slides: Introduction to Linear Algebra

## Slide 1: Linear Algebra Overview
- Linear algebra is the mathematics of vectors and matrices.
- Applications in physics, engineering, data science, and ML.
- Reference Video: [MIT OCW: Vectors and Spaces](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZK3O402wf1c).

---

## Slide 2: Vectors
- Scalars: A single number.
- Vectors: A list of numbers.
- Example: A 2D vector can be \([x, y]\).

---

## Slide 3: Vector Operations
- Addition:
  \\[\\mathbf{u} + \\mathbf{v} \\to \\text{Tip-to-Tail Rule.}\\]
- Scalar Multiplication:
  \\[c \\cdot \\mathbf{v} \\to \\text{Scaling vector magnitude.}\\]

---

## Slide 4: Dot Product
- Formula:
  \\\[\\mathbf{u} \\cdot \\mathbf{v} = \\| \\mathbf{u} \\| \\| \\mathbf{v} \\| \\cos{\\theta}\\]
- Applications:
  - Angle calculations.
  - Projections.
